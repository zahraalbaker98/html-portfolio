About Me

Hi! My name is Zahra.

Raised in Portugal in an Iraqi family, I have always been curious about different cultures, ideas and opportunities. That curiosity led me to study Marketing in Edinburgh for my Bachelor's Degree, spend a year in Bologna through the Erasmus programme and build a career across marketing, cybersecurity and education.

Today, I work as a Sales and Admissions Officer at a school while pursuing a new challenge in web development through App Brewery's Web Development Course. This portfolio showcases my projects and my journey into tech. While some of these projects may be beginner-level, each one represents a new skill learned, a challenge overcome and a step forward in my growth. I am very excited to keep learning, building and improving along the way!

Outside of work and studying, you will usually find me reading on my kindle (one of my best purchases), cooking for friends and family, painting or making ceramics. I also love to travel and going to concerts whenever I get the chance!
